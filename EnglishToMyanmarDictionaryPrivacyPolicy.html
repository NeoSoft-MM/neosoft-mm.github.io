Privacy Policy

English to Myanmar Dictionary

Last updated: November 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the English to Myanmar Dictionary mobile application (“the App”) handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This App does not collect, share, or store any personal data.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. All features of the dictionary, including search history and word bookmarks, operate entirely on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

Any data you create (such as saved words or preferences) is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to any server.

3. Permissions

The App may request permission to send local notifications (e.g., “Word of the Day” reminders). These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external data collection or transmission.

4. Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality. If future updates include optional online features, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes are made indicates acceptance of the new policy.
